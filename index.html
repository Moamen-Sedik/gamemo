Welcome to The X and O game,
commonly known as Tic-Tac-Toe, is a classic and straightforward two-player game played on a 3x3 grid.
Players take turns marking empty spaces with their designated symbol (X or O),
aiming to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row of three marks.
A simple yet engaging game, it encourages strategic thinking and is often enjoyed for its quick, accessible gameplay.
Enjoy the game :)
