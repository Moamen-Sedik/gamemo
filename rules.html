1. Two players take turns marking empty spaces on a 3x3 grid.

2. Player 1 is X, Player 2 is O.

3. The goal is to get three of your marks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

4. Players alternate turns until a win, a draw, or the board is full.

5. No moves are allowed in occupied spaces.

6. The first to achieve three in a row wins.
